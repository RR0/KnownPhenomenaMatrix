Alexander naît en 1946 à Hull (Nord-Est de l'Angleterre).

En 1951, alors qu'il vient d'arriver à l'école, il ressent soudain une vive inquiétude quant à sa mère, durant toute la matinée. En rentrant chez lui pour le déjeuner il s'apercevra qu'elle s'est gravement coupé le petit doigt.

En 1967, son frère achète un fameux livre de croyance spiritualiste On the Edge of the Etheric, 1932 Il a 1 frère et 2 soeurs all of whom are involved in my mediumship and two of whom have sat in my home circle for many years. Throughout my childhood I had many experiences which may be described as Psychic the first of which is still very vivid in my memory. Until I was 21 years of age I had no knowledge of Spiritualism. I am married and have two sons neither of whom are involved in Spiritualism.
